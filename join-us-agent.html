KW CAP RATE SYSTEM

Keller Williams commission structure sets the CAP RATE system (upper limit). Each office has a cap on commissions based on economic conditions and operating expenses for that specific market center. Once an agent reaches the set amount of CAP RATE, the agent is at a 100% commission until their anniversary year starts again. This structure allows agents to make more income than with any other company. This very competitive commission split system makes Keller Williams the largest real estate company in the world.
